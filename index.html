Investment Summary

This is an opportunity to invest in the preferred equities tied to a Hotel use development project located at 38-65 11th Street, Long Island City, NY 11101 (the “Property”.) The Property is situated to the north of the Queensboro Bridge in Long Island City, Queens, NY (the “Neighborhood”.) The subject land is 11,425+/- square feet and the maximum buildable area is 57,125+/- square feet. This site currently consists of a one-story industrial property. Our team, Eastone Capital, serves as the developer of this project, already acquired the land parcel of the Property at the sale price of $199,058 on July 23, 2013 and we planned to develop a Hotel with parking garage.
